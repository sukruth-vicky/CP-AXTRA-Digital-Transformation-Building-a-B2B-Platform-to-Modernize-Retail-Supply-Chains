Retail supply chains serving small businesses are often fragmented, inefficient, and heavily reliant on offline processes. CP AXTRA, one of Southeast Asia’s largest wholesale and retail operators, partnered with McKinsey & Company to build a new digital platform that would modernize how small businesses source supplies and improve supply chain resilience.

CP AXTRA serves hundreds of thousands of restaurants, cafés, and hotels that rely on frequent supply purchases. Traditionally, procurement required physical store visits, limiting convenience, transparency, and operational efficiency. The COVID-19 pandemic further exposed the need for a scalable digital solution that could support uninterrupted supply access.

McKinsey helped CP AXTRA design and launch a B2B digital platform that allowed customers to browse inventory, place orders, and manage supply procurement through a unified digital interface. The platform integrated logistics, inventory management, and customer analytics to provide real-time visibility and streamlined fulfillment.

The transformation involved building a new digital business model, developing scalable technology infrastructure, and redesigning operating processes to support omnichannel supply fulfillment. The platform also enabled CP AXTRA to capture valuable customer insights, improve demand forecasting, and optimize inventory distribution.

The impact was substantial. The digital platform accelerated CP AXTRA’s transition into a tech-enabled retail leader, enabling faster ordering cycles, improved inventory efficiency, and stronger customer engagement. It also supported supply continuity during disruptions and strengthened operational resilience.

Measured impact included:

• More than 350,000 businesses gained improved access to digital procurement tools
• Online sales increased significantly, contributing to overall digital growth
• Procurement cycle times reduced by approximately 20–30 percent
• Improved supply chain resilience and inventory visibility
• Enhanced operational efficiency and customer experience

This case demonstrates how building a digital platform can transform traditional retail models, enabling scalability, resilience, and sustained growth.
